Battle Scars: Action Movies That Leave a Mark

These action-packed stories deliver more than thrills—they’ll leave you breathless and broken.

1. Southpaw

This movie follows a boxer who has to rebuild his life after a tradegy.

2. Saving Private Ryan

Tom Hanks stars in this World War II movie of a team on a dangerous mission.

3. Hacksaw Ridge

This faith based movie is a true story of soldier Desmond Dos, who saved countless lives without a weapon.

4. Logan

Logan meets a young mutant just like him, a perfect end to the Wolverine movies!

5. End of Watch

Two LAPD Officers whose strong brotherhood is tested as they uncover dangerous secrets while patrolling Los Angeles.
