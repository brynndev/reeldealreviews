Fun Fact

The idea came from watching TV! According to author Suzanne Collins, she came up with the idea for the book whilst channel hopping between reality TV and news coverage, and it made her think about the way in which news of war and crime has become entertainment, and the two concepts blurred in her head because she was tired, creating the idea for the books.

Character Highlight
Finnick Odair is the Capitol’s charming victor with a fighter’s heart. Beneath his polished image lies a survivor shaped by loss, loyalty, and quiet bravery. His skill, honesty, and devotion to Annie make him one of the trilogy’s most unforgettable heroes.
Iconic Outfit
Cinna’s “Girl on Fire” dress is one of the most striking looks in the series. The fitted black gown bursts into vibrant, controlled flames, turning Katniss into a living symbol of power and defiance. In a single moment, she captures Panem’s attention—transforming from an overlooked tribute into the face of something bigger than the Games.
