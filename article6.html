Fun Fact

The fourth installation, On Stranger Tides, was the most expensive film ever made at the time, not adjusted for inflation. The budget ran to $300,000,000. That's more than the budget of all three Lord Of The Rings films combined.

Character Highlight
Captain Jack Sparrow is the most iconic character in the series, known for his wit, unpredictability, and unforgettable charm.
Iconic Outfit
Nothing says Gothic Romance quite like a billowing white nightgown. Even if the first Pirates of the Caribbean is ultimately an action-adventure, the ghost story tinges make it appropriate from some genre-y costuming. Elizabeth spends a good amount of time in this nightgown and what a lovely nightgown it is.
