Fun Fact

Kristen Stewart had to wear contacts in the films. In the books, Bella is famously described as having brown eyes. Kirsten Stewart, however, has green eyes, which meant she had to wear contacts throughout the films! She also dons a red pair when her character turns into a vampire. Hopefully they weren't too itchy!

Best Song From The Series

“A Thousand Years”

"A Thousand Years" by Christina Perri captures the soft, timeless, emotional vibe of Bella and Edward’s relationship, and it became the musical identity of the finale—romantic, dramatic, and totally Twilight-coded.

Character Highlight
Alice Cullen is the stylish, future-seeing heartbeat of the Cullen family. Her optimism, loyalty, and sharp instincts make her one of the saga’s most unforgettable characters.
Iconic Outfit
Bella’s cobalt-blue halter dress is one of her most iconic looks, paired with a cream embroidered cardigan for effortless elegance. Even with a leg brace, she stays true to her style by wearing a Converse and comfy Capri leggings under the dress.
