Peak 2000s Romance

From meet-cutes to makeover montages, these 2000s gems still have our hearts.

1. She's The Man

Amanda Bynes disguises herself as her twin brother to play on his school’s boys’ soccer team, leading to romance and comedic chaos.

2. The Proposal

This classic movie follows a high-powered book editor who forces her assistant into a fake engagement to avoid deportation, leading to unexpected romance.

3. Made of Honor

McDreamy is a man who realizes he’s in love with his best friend and tries to win her heart before her wedding.

4. How To Lose a Guy in 10 Days

This rom‑com is about a writer who tries to drive a man away for an article while he bets he can make her fall in love with him.

5. 13 Going on 30

Jennifer Garner is a teenage girl who magically wakes up as a 30-year-old woman and navigates adult life while rediscovering her true self.
