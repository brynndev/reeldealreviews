Fright Night Favorites

Your go-to guide for the most scream-worthy, spellbinding, and nostalgic Halloween movies ever made.

1. Scream

This is a slasher film where a masked killer terrorizes a small town, blending horror with dark humor and mystery.

2. Halloween

Jamie Lee Curtis and Michael Myers, a masked killer who escapes from a mental institution to stalk and terrorize his hometown on Halloween night.

3. Beetlejuice

A dark comedy about a recently deceased couple who enlist a mischievous ghost to scare away the new living inhabitants of their home.

4. Carrie

This horror film follows a shy high school girl with telekinetic powers who unleashes revenge on her tormentors after a cruel prank at prom.

5. Annabelle

This is a horror film about a doll possessed by a malevolent spirit that brings terror and tragedy to the family that owns it.
