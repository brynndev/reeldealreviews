The Disney Classics That Never Grow Old

Relive the wonder, laughter, and songs that defined your childhood

1. Freaky Friday

A classic comedy in which a mother and daughter switch bodies and must live each other’s lives, learning to understand and appreciate one another.

2. The Princess Diaries

This movie follows a shy teenager who suddenly learns she’s the heir to a royal throne and must adjust to her new life as a princess.

3. The Parent Trap

This one tells the story of identical twin sisters, separated at birth, who meet at summer camp and scheme to reunite their divorced parents.

4. A Cinderella Story

A high school girl who, under a secret identity, falls for a popular boy online while navigating family pressures and personal dreams.

5. The Game Plan

Dwayne Johnson, a professional football player whose life is turned upside down when he discovers he has an 8-year-old daughter.
